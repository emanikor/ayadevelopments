Aya City

Aya City is an exclusive seaside community in the Medici Principality. It is a lucrative business city, with its HQ holding almost all of Aya Trades LLC’s businesses. Aya City is a budding metropole, which maximizes the space of this beautiful coastal plot, as it offers up to 500 units to rent. The city’s architecture attests to one of the brand’s values of blending the narrative between work and play, as aside from being a business hub, it holds several play-to-earn games in Lootverse. From being an F1 racer to a powerful god battling evil, residents of Aya City have all the entertainment their hearts desire.
